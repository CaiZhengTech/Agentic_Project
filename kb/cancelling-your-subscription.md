# Cancelling your subscription

## How to cancel

1. Go to Account → Billing → Cancel Subscription.
2. Select a reason (optional but helps us improve) and confirm.
3. Cancellation takes effect at the **end of your current billing cycle** — you keep full
   access to your plan until then, and you will not be charged again.
4. You'll receive an email confirmation with the exact date access ends.

## What happens to your data

- Your account and data remain accessible (read-only for exports) for 30 days after
  cancellation, so you can export anything you need — see "Exporting your data and backups."
- After 30 days, account data is permanently deleted and cannot be recovered. If you think
  you might reactivate later, export your data before this window closes regardless.
- API keys and any dedicated IP allocation are released at the moment access ends, not at the
  30-day mark — update any external integrations before your cancellation date.

## Reactivating

You can reactivate anytime before the 30-day data-retention window closes by going to
Account → Billing → Reactivate — this restores your previous plan and data with no gap in
history. After 30 days, reactivating means starting a new account from scratch.

## Multi-seat accounts

Cancelling the account cancels all seats. To remove individual users while keeping the
account active, use Account → Team → Manage Seats instead of full cancellation.

## When to contact support

Contact support if: you cancelled by mistake and it's within the 30-day window (we can
reactivate immediately), you were charged after your access-end date, or you need to
cancel but a required approval step (e.g., admin sign-off on a multi-seat account) is
blocking you.
