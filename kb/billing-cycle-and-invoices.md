# Understanding your billing cycle and invoices

## Billing cycle basics

Northbeam bills monthly, in advance, on the anniversary of your signup date. For example, if
you signed up on the 14th, you're billed on the 14th of every month for the upcoming month.
Annual billing (10% discount) is available on Pro and Enterprise — contact sales to switch.

## Finding your invoices

All invoices are available under Account → Billing → Invoice History, going back to account
creation. Each invoice can be downloaded as a PDF. Invoices are also emailed automatically to
your account's billing contact at the time of charge.

## Understanding charges on your invoice

- **Base plan fee**: your monthly plan cost (Basic/Pro/Enterprise).
- **Seat charges**: if your account has multiple users, each active seat is billed pro-rata
  for partial months (added mid-cycle) or in full for complete months.
- **Add-ons**: any additional services (e.g., extra storage) appear as separate line items.
- **Proration credits**: shown as negative line items when you upgrade/downgrade mid-cycle
  (see "Upgrading or downgrading your plan").

## Common billing questions

- **Why was I charged before canceling?** Cancellation stops future billing but does not
  refund the current cycle unless you're within the refund window — see "Refunds and
  hardware returns policy."
- **My card was declined**: update your payment method under Account → Billing → Payment
  Methods. We retry failed charges automatically for 5 days before suspending service.
- **I was double-charged**: this is rare but can happen with payment method changes mid-cycle
  overlapping with a retry. Support can identify and refund duplicate charges.

## When to contact support

Contact support with the invoice number (visible at the top of each PDF) if: a charge doesn't
match your expected plan/seats, you believe you were double-charged, or a payment method
update isn't reflected on your next invoice.
