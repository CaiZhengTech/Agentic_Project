# Upgrading or downgrading your plan

See "What each plan includes (Basic / Pro / Enterprise)" for the full feature breakdown
before switching.

## Upgrading

1. Go to Account → Billing → Change Plan and select the new tier (Basic → Pro, or Pro →
   Enterprise).
2. Upgrades take effect immediately — you'll have access to the new plan's features
   (e.g., priority_vpn_support, api_access, data_export on Pro; dedicated_ip,
   custom_integrations on Enterprise) within a few minutes.
3. You're charged a prorated amount for the remainder of the current billing cycle at the
   new plan's rate; your next full invoice reflects the new plan price going forward.
4. Upgrading from Basic to Enterprise directly is supported — you don't need to go through
   Pro first.

## Downgrading

1. Go to Account → Billing → Change Plan and select the lower tier.
2. Downgrades take effect at the **start of your next billing cycle**, not immediately — you
   keep your current plan's features until then, so there's no service interruption
   mid-cycle.
3. Any feature exclusive to your current tier (e.g., a dedicated IP on Enterprise) is
   released back to the pool at downgrade time. If you're relying on a dedicated IP for
   firewall allowlisting, update those configurations before your downgrade takes effect.
4. No refund is issued for the difference between plans during the remainder of the current
   cycle — see "Refunds and hardware returns policy" for exceptions.

## Multi-seat accounts

Plan changes apply to the whole account, not individual seats. To move only some users to a
higher tier, contact sales about split-tier or custom Enterprise arrangements.

## When to contact support

Contact support if an upgrade doesn't unlock the expected features within 15 minutes, or a
scheduled downgrade doesn't take effect at the start of the new cycle. For questions about
which plan fits your needs, see "Contacting sales and pre-sales questions."
