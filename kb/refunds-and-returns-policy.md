# Refunds and hardware returns policy

## Subscription refunds

- **Within 14 days of first signup**: full refund of your first invoice, no questions asked.
  Request this via Account → Billing → Request Refund, or through support.
- **After 14 days**: subscription charges are non-refundable for the current billing cycle,
  but cancellation prevents any future charges (see "Cancelling your subscription").
- **Billed in error** (e.g., duplicate charge, charged after cancellation processed):
  refundable regardless of the 14-day window — this is a billing correction, not a
  discretionary refund.
- **Annual plans**: refundable on a prorated basis for unused full months remaining, minus
  any discount applied for choosing annual billing, within the first 30 days only.

## Hardware returns

If your plan includes Northbeam-provided hardware (e.g., VPN access tokens, network
appliances for Enterprise accounts):

1. Hardware may be returned within 30 days of delivery for a full refund if unopened, or
   within 14 days if opened and functional.
2. Defective hardware can be returned any time within the 1-year warranty period — see
   "Filing a hardware warranty claim" instead of the standard return process.
3. Request a return label under Account → Hardware → Request Return. Ship within 10 days of
   generating the label, or it expires and must be regenerated.
4. Refunds process within 5–7 business days of us receiving the returned item.

## What's not covered

- Usage-based add-on charges already consumed (e.g., API calls already made) are not
  refundable.
- Hardware damaged from misuse (not a manufacturing defect) is not covered by the return
  policy — see the warranty doc for the distinction.

## When to contact support

Contact support if: you're past a stated window but believe your case qualifies as billed-in-
error, a return label doesn't generate, or a refund hasn't posted 10+ business days after we
confirmed receipt of a hardware return.
