# Filing a hardware warranty claim

Northbeam-provided hardware (VPN tokens, network appliances) carries a 1-year manufacturer
warranty against defects from the date of delivery.

## What's covered

- Manufacturing defects: device fails to power on, ports fail, internal component failure
  under normal use.
- Premature wear unrelated to physical damage (e.g., a token's battery failing well before
  its rated life).

## What's not covered

- Physical damage from drops, liquid, or crushing.
- Damage from unauthorized modification or use outside the device's specifications.
- Cosmetic wear (scratches, discoloration) that doesn't affect function.
- Devices past the 1-year warranty window — see "Hardware returns" in the refunds policy for
  general return options, which don't apply once the return window has also closed.

## Filing a claim

1. Go to Account → Hardware → File Warranty Claim and select the affected device (matched by
   serial number, found on the device or your original order confirmation).
2. Describe the defect and, if possible, attach a photo or short video showing the issue.
3. We may ask you to run a basic diagnostic (device-specific reset or test sequence) before
   approving the claim, to rule out a configuration issue rather than hardware failure.
4. Approved claims ship a replacement device within 3–5 business days, along with a prepaid
   return label for the defective unit. Send the defective unit back within 14 days of
   receiving the replacement, or you may be charged for it.

## Claim status and delays

Check claim status under Account → Hardware → Warranty Claims. Most claims are
approved/denied within 2 business days of submission; diagnostic requests add time until you
respond.

## When to contact support

Contact support if: a claim has been pending review for more than 3 business days, a
replacement hasn't shipped within 5 business days of approval, or you disagree with a denied
claim and want it reviewed.
